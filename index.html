Features

Link and news sharing

Users can share the links that they found interesting and useful on your platform and start a discussion about that.

Discussions and Comments

Anyone in your platform can write a comment to any post shared on the platform and others can reply to that comment. This is how discussions work.

Upvote & Downvote

If a user found useful a post shared on the platform, they can show their appreciation by upvoting that post. Not every post may be welcomed by your community. If this is the case, users can react to that post by downvoting it. There are some limitations to downvote a post. Click here to get more info about that.

Have full control on who to join your community

Not everyone can join your community. You have full control of the signup process. You can invite users. The platform provides you an admin panel to do that.

Ask or show something

Anyone asking for advice about anything can submit a question to the community and start collecting feedback. Just finished a fantastic project? Projects can be shown to the community and collected all needed feedback to improve it for the better.

Unique domain to your platform

Once you create your platform, you get a unique subdomain automatically created for your platform. If you would like to use your own custom domain to access your platform, you can do that as well.
